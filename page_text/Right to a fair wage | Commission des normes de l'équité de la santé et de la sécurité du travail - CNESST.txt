Right to a fair wage


Pay equity is the right of workers in a typical female job to receive pay equal to a person in a typical male job of equivalent value in the same company.




What is pay equity?


Pay equity aims to correct differences in pay caused by gender discrimination within a company. 




What does gender discrimination mean?


A better understanding of gender discrimination allows jobs to be assessed fairly and wage differences prevented.
